# Statistical Calculator in C

Statistical calculator in C to compute all Measures of Central Tendency and Measures of Dispersion for individual, discrete, and continuous series.

---

## How to use it

---

### Step 1
First choose the series type:
- Enter **1** for Individual series  
- Enter **2** for Discrete series  
- Enter **3** for Continuous series  

Then enter the series values.

---

### Step 2
Choose what you want to calculate by entering the corresponding number from the menu.

---

### Step 3
If you want to continue with the same series, enter **y**.
